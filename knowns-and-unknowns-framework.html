Knows and Unknowns Framework
(...) NASA reported that they were using the framework as part of risk analysis in space missions, especially to uncover Unknown Unknowns. In a presentation from 2003, we can see how they used the framework to evaluate the risks in the return journey of the Space Shuttle. They concentrate on moving the unknowns to the known realm.
